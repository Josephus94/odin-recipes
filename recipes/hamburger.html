Hamburger

Ingredients

  • American Cheese
  • Mayonneaise
  • Ground beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Buns (2)

Procedure

  1. Cook really good the ground beef, use oil and put a pisk of salt
  2. Cut the tomatoes and lettuce, make sure ingrediets are fresh and clean
  3. Spread the mayo on the buns, and heat them up just for 5 seconds.
  4. Stack the ingredients one by one making sure the beef was well cooked.
  5. If the meal is for you go ahead and enjoy feller.
