Krabby Patty

Ingredients

  • A teaspoon of raspberry
  • Seaweed Sauce

  • Dried Kelp
  • Squid Ink
  • Sea Pickles
  • Sea Cheese

  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

  5. Apply mustard and add two rings of onion.
  6. After following the steps cautiously you finished!!
