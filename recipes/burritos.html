Burritos


The best recipe to make the best burritos will blow your mind, and will have those taste buds exploding.

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortillas
  • Cooked pork
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Beans

Procedure

  1. Heat the flour tortillas and the pork put it in a pan and heat it up.
  2. Chop the veggies, and prepare a good suace, heat the beans.
  3. Input the cheese pork and beans into a Tortilla and roll.
  4. Put the burritos and tortilla into a plate.
  5. Enjoy!
