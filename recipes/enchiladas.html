Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • Vegatables if you like
  • Cooked chicken
  • Red sauce
  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • oil

Procedure

  1. Heat up the tortillas in a pan, if you like you can put some oil so you can partially fry them.
  2. Mix all ingredients for the sauce mix them and once the sauce is ready heat it up on a pan.
  3. Shred the Cheese, and get the cooked chicken in a pan preheat it. (Cheese will go in the tortilla))
  4. Put the heated sauce in a pan, get the heated tortillas and dip them completly on the sauce.
  5. Put the cheese and the chicken in the tortilla and roll.
  6. Enjoy!
